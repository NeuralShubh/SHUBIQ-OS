# SHUBIQ OS

SHUBIQ OS is a modern, all-in-one business operating system for founders and small teams. It brings your daily operations into a single command center so you can manage clients, projects, revenue, and expenses with clarity.

## What It’s For

SHUBIQ OS helps you run your business end-to-end:

- **Projects** — track active projects, budgets, paid amounts, remaining balance, and progress.
- **Clients** — manage client profiles, contact details, and status.
- **Documents** — create and manage invoices, quotations, and proposals.
- **Income** — track revenue and payments from invoices, including partial payments.
- **Expenses** — record company spending (Studio, Labs, and Overall).
- **Products (Labs)** — manage product pricing, plans, and revenue.
- **Analytics** — view performance trends, splits, and business health insights.
- **Dashboard** — see key metrics, outstanding payments, and recent activity in one place.
- **Settings** — configure business profile, tax rate, currency, and document details.

## Who It’s For

SHUBIQ OS is built for founders, agencies, freelancers, and small teams who need a clean, premium interface to manage operations without juggling multiple tools.

## Key Benefits

- Single workspace for all business operations
- Fast document creation and payment tracking
- Clear visibility into revenue, expenses, and outstanding amounts
- Premium UI in both dark and light modes
- Mobile-friendly experience for on-the-go access
